What Leaders Ask Us

Safe Workspace is a cloud-based desktop environment built on Azure Virtual Desktop or Citrix Cloud. It unifies Microsoft 365, endpoint security, monitoring, compliance automation, and support into a single platform managed entirely by Trisian. The principle is straightforward: when technology works perfectly, leaders are free to focus on what drives their business forward.
Through our structured three-pillar assessment, we score your organization across security, performance, and compliance maturity. Combined with your user count, industry, and regulatory requirements, this data-driven approach produces a tier recommendation with full transparency. The tier is determined by your assessment results — ensuring the best fit for your organization.
Three components: a per-user Platform Fee covering all automated services and Microsoft 365 licensing, contracted Support Hours for human-delivered services with every minute logged, and separately quoted Professional Services for project-based work. Azure infrastructure is billed at cost with zero markup.
Advanced and Enterprise clients receive 24/7 SLA coverage built into their base rate — after-hours support is included at the same cost. All tiers have access to Emergency Escalation through designated Authorized Contacts, targeting 15-minute engagement with automated escalation workflows.
Our standard implementation follows a 90-day structured plan: Days 1-30 for platform deployment and pilot group, Days 31-60 for full migration and training, Days 61-90 for optimization, compliance validation, and steady-state transition. Timeline scales with environment complexity.
