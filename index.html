Frequently Asked Questions

What Leaders Ask Us

What exactly is Safe Workspace?

+

Safe Workspace is a cloud-based desktop environment (DaaS) built on Azure Virtual Desktop or Citrix Cloud. It replaces fragmented systems with a unified, secure, and compliance-ready digital ecosystem — including Microsoft 365, endpoint security, monitoring, and automated governance, all managed by Trisian.

Which compliance frameworks do you support?

+

Our platform supports HIPAA, SOC 2 Type II, PCI-DSS, GDPR, SOX, DORA, CMMC 2.0, GLBA, and FERPA with pre-built technical controls, automated evidence collection, and compliance posture dashboards. The Enterprise tier provides the most comprehensive compliance automation.

How do you determine which tier is right for us?

+

Through our structured assessment process, we score your organization across all three pillars — security, performance, and compliance. This data-driven approach, combined with your user count, industry, and regulatory requirements, determines the optimal tier recommendation with full transparency.

What happens during an emergency at 2 AM?

+

Advanced and Enterprise clients have 24/7 SLA coverage included. All tiers have access to Emergency Escalation — your designated contacts call our emergency line for immediate, queue-jumping response targeting 15-minute engagement. Our HaloPSA system auto-notifies on-call engineers instantly.

Do you replace our existing Microsoft 365?

+

Each tier includes Microsoft 365 licensing (Business Premium, E3, or E5). If you already have M365 licenses, we offer a License Transition Credit framework to minimize overlap costs during migration. We handle the complete transition seamlessly.

How long does deployment take?

+

Our standard implementation follows a structured 90-day plan: Days 1-30 for platform deployment and initial onboarding, Days 31-60 for full user migration and training, and Days 61-90 for optimization, compliance validation, and transition to steady state. Complexity may adjust this timeline.
