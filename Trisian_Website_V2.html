Transparent by Design

Three Components. No Surprises.

Our billing structure has three distinct components, each with defined scope — so you always know what you are paying for before any work begins.

Platform Fee

Per-user monthly. Everything that runs automatically is included — from monitoring to patching to security updates to licensing.

Support Hours

Contracted minimum for human-delivered services. Full visibility into every hour — logged, categorized, and reported monthly with SLA-backed response times.

Professional Services

Migrations, security implementations, compliance programs — scoped projects with defined deliverables, timelines, and outcomes. Quoted separately.

Azure Infrastructure at Cost — Azure compute, storage, and networking are billed as a pass-through with zero markup. You retain full visibility into consumption. We optimize your cloud spend, we do not profit from it.

Optional Add-Ons — Compliance log archival (up to 7+ years), advanced user experience monitoring, SIEM/SOAR, email threat protection, and emergency readiness retainers — available without changing tiers.
